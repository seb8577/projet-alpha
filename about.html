ABOUT ME

I discovered photography five years ago by landscapes captures. But it was especially during a trip to South Africa that I took a passion for animal photography. Capturing animal beauty and trying to recreate moments of wild life are exciting times for me. To show patience, to seek the right angle and the good light, "to play" with the animal are moments of happiness. I then tweak my technique during a photo safari in northern Tanzania, simply grandiose, magical! Next destination ..? Most certainly Namibia or Kenya.
