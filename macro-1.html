WATER DROP

A drop is a small amount of liquid where the surface tension is important. The smallest analyzed water droplet with the number of 3 x 1020 water molecules remains a giant superstructure at the molecular scale. It is the forces involved (weight, surface tension, inertia for a moving drop) that determine its shape.
