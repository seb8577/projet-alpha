SERENGETI

Serengeti National Park is the only park in Tanzania where cheetahs and leopards can easily be seen during photo safaris. There is very little chance of seeing these two species in the other parks. The leopard is a population extremely difficult to identify because the animal is very discreet. He camouflages himself and lives in trees and rocks.

TARANGIRE

Tarangire National Park is an important national park in northern Tanzania. It owes its name to the river Tarangire which crosses it. The park surrounds the valley of the Tarangire River. It has an elongated shape, nearly 100 km long and average 30 wide. Its average altitude is 1,100 meters.
